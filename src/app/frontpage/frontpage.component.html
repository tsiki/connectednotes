What is Connected Notes

It's an open source note taking app based on a modern version of the Zettelkasten system.

We all study to be able to use the information we study later. For this, we need to take the notes we've written before and connect them to each other. This is where Zettelkasten comes in.

The notes are stored in your Google Drive (more cloud storage options coming later).

Features

Privacy

We don’t store your notes - we simply connect to your Google Drive (more cloud services are on the way) and store them there. What you write is between your cloud storage and you.

Everywhere

Access your notes from phone or laptop. Notes are synchronized across devices.

Reliability

We don’t want you to worry about losing your notes if a fledgling startup goes under or a domain name expires - your notes are stored where you have complete control over them.

No lock-in

Everything is stored as markdown files, in plain text. Don't like the service? Just download the notes from your Google Drive and transfer them to another service.

Open source

Any note service should be in it for the long haul. In our mind, being open source is a necessary part of that.
